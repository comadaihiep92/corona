Your Grocery is in safe hands

Hygienically packed. Safely delivered

  • Zero touch delivery option
  • Regular cleaning of warehouses
  • Fever screening of all executives

We are following the WHO recommended measures to help control the spread of coronavirus and to ensure the safe delivery of your groceries

  • Our staff has been trained to follow the best hygiene practices.
  • All delivery partners have been provided with sanitizers and masks. All masks are being replaced every 2 days to ensure the sanitary value of the safety gear.
  • Our inventory crates are being washed and disinfected regularly.
  • We are providing zero touch delivery option for all our customers to ensure contact-free and safe delivery of groceries.
  • We have ensured mandatory screening of the staff at all our warehouses using infrared contactless thermometers.
  • All employees & delivery partners have been advised to stay at home if they show any symptoms and to get in touch with us for further medical assistance.

We are making some important decisions

What to do?

  • Practice frequent hand washing. Wash hands with soap and water or use alcohol based hand rub. Wash hands even if they are visibly clean.
  • Cover your nose and mouth with handkerchief/tissue while sneezing and coughing.
  • Throw used tissues into closed bins immediately after use.
  • See a doctor if you feel unwell (fever, difficulty in breathing and cough). While visiting the doctor wear a mask /cloth to cover your mouth & nose.
  • If you have these symptoms call State helpline number or Ministry of Health & Family Welfare's 24X7 helpline at 011-23978046.
  • Avoid participating in large gatherings.

What to do?

  • Don't have close contact with anyone. if you're experiencing cough and fever.
  • Don't touch your eyes. nose and mouth.
  • Don't spit in public.
